TRAVEL POLICY

 

 

Objective:

 

Managers, Staff and Associates of Sumi Motherson Group are required to undertake inland travel for business purposes. A need has been felt to rationalise the policy on mode of travel, rates for boarding and lodging for different cities in the Country. The policy has been formulated based on the prevailing rates and cost of living and on the feed back given by frequent travellers in the Group.

 

Travel Policy Travel for Business & Training

        These entitlements will apply to all employees travelling on official work for a period of less than 15 days.

        Policy on travel for a period exceeding 15 days and for special projects, would be dealt with on case-to-case basis by the President of the company.

 

Approving Authority

All travel plans are to be approved by the respective Presidents / Unit Heads, as the case may be.

 

Mode of Travel

  • All employees of grade Asst. General Manager and above shall, while travelling on official work, be eligible for travel by air. Where, however, alternative modes of travel are convenient and cost effective, employees are expected to travel by the same.
  • Employees from Asst. Manager to Senior Manager Level shall travel by AC 2 tier / AC Chair/I class - Rail.
  • Executive, Staff and all other employees shall travel by II sleeper 3-tier train.
  • Air travel in all cases will be by economy class.
  • Travel by mode/class other than specified above may be allowed by specific written permission of the President.

 

Accommodation

  • Wherever guest house accommodation is available, employees on travel should avail of the guest house facilities as a first option of boarding and lodging.
  • Employees requiring hotel accommodation while travelling on official work will be entitled to stay in a standard single occupancy room.
  • General Manager and above to be reimbursed for hotel accommodation on actual basis.
  • AGM to DGM to be reimbursed on actual basis subject to a maximum of 3 star hotel fare.
  • Others to be reimbursed on actual basis subject to the following limits per day.

Per Diem (Allowance in lieu of availing hotel facilities)

All employees will be admissible to per diem allowance at the rates given above for meals, boarding and incidental expenses, whenever employees make their own arrangement for lodging. GM and above would also be entitled to Rs. 500/- per diem allowance, wherever applicable.

 

Guest House Accommodation

  • Assistant Manager and above, on travel to outstation will stay in Company Guest House accommodation where they will be provided Boarding, Lodging and Laundry. However they will be entitled to Rs. 100/- per day for incidental expenses.
  • Executive, Staff and Associates will also stay in Guest House accommodation in stations where such facilities are available. They will also be provided Boarding and Lodging and Rs. 50/- per day as incidental expenses.
  • All reservations in Guest House would be made by MATA. Requests will be sent at least three days before the requirement and MATA will confirm the reservation.

 

Local Conveyance During Business Travel

        Assistant General Manager and above will be reimbursed on actuals of hiring private taxis / metered taxis.

        Manager to Sr. Manager will be reimbursed on actuals of hiring metered taxis.

        Assistant Manager to Deputy Manager, Executive and Staff will be reimbursed on actuals of hiring autorickshaws.

        Associates will be entitled reimbursement of actual bus fare.

         Depending upon the urgency of work, exigencies of duties, to carry bulk documents, materials and equipment, deviation to the policy may be allowed by the President on a case to case basis.
