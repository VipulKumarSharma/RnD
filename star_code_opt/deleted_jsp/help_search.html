Search By

 

This page allows a User to search Travel requisitions on the basis of requisition number or origiantor.

  1. Requisition number- To search the requisition on the basis of requisition number,the user has to select the radio button "Requisition number" and enter the keywords that must be there in the requisition number ,in the field "Enter your keywords" and finally click on SEARCH button. .

  2. Originator- To search the requisition on the basis of originator,the user has to select the radio button "Originator" and enter the keywords that must be there in the originator name ,in the field "Enter your keywords" and finally click on SEARCH button.
